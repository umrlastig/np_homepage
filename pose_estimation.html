6-DoF Pose Estimation

Estimation de pose

73 avenue de Paris · 94165 Saint-Mandé CEDEX · nathan.piasco@ign.fr

In this work we present an online camera pose estimation method that combines Content-Based Image Retrieval (CBIR) and pose refinement based on a learned representation of the scene geometry extracted from monocular images. Our pose estimation method is two-step, we first retrieve an initial 6 Degrees of Freedom (DoF) location of an unknown-pose query by retrieving the most similar candidate in a pool of geo-referenced images. In a second time, we refine the query pose with a Perspective-n-Point (PnP) algorithm where the 3D points are obtained thanks to a generated depth map from the retrieved image candidate.
